How long does the probate process usually take in Queens, NY?
The probate process in Queens and throughout New York City typically takes between 7 months to over a year, depending on factors such as the size of the estate, whether there are any disputes among beneficiaries, and how quickly court documents are processed. Simple estates with no conflicts and complete documentation can sometimes be completed in as little as 6 to 9 months. Our firm works proactively to minimize delays and keeps you updated on each stage of the process.

What is the process for starting probate with your firm if my loved one passed away in New York?
To begin the probate process, you’ll first schedule a consultation with our firm. We’ll review the decedent’s will (if one exists), discuss the estate’s assets, and explain the next steps. We’ll help you gather the necessary documents, such as the death certificate and an original will. From there, we prepare and file the probate petition with the Queens Surrogate's Court or the court in the appropriate New York county. Our team handles court appearances, notifies interested parties, addresses debts and claims, and helps distribute assets to beneficiaries efficiently and compassionately.
